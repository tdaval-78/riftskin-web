Download and install on macOS

Use the simple installer bundle if you want the easiest path. It copies RiftSkin into Applications and removes the quarantine flag.

Download simple installer Download app only Open releases

How it works

  • Unzip the downloaded archive
  • Double click install.command to copy RiftSkin into Applications
  • If macOS asks, enter your administrator password
  • On first launch, if macOS blocks the app, right click RiftSkin and choose Open, then Open again
  • If needed, go to System Settings, Privacy & Security, then click Open Anyway
