Frequently asked questions

Which files can I import?

Use skin packages supported by RiftSkin import workflow.

Do I need to select default skin in game?

Yes. For replacement to apply, select the champion's default skin in-game.

How do I reset my password?

Go to My account, enter your email, and use Forgot password.

Does RiftSkin support Windows?

Current public workflow is focused on macOS app distribution and account-based access.

Who can see my account data?

Account auth is handled through Supabase services. See legal and privacy section for details.
