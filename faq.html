Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every account gets 7 days free. After that, access is 20 EUR/month.

What does subscription unlock?

Active subscription unlocks RiftSkin software access and related account-based features.

Which files can I import?

Use skin packages supported by RiftSkin import workflow. Follow the in-app instruction page for exact supported formats.

Do I need to select default skin in game?

Yes. For replacement to apply, select the champion's default skin in-game as instructed in RiftSkin.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscription can be canceled anytime from your billing portal once configured.

How do I reset my password?

Go to My account, enter your email, and use “Forgot password”.

Does RiftSkin support Windows?

Current public workflow is focused on macOS app distribution and account-based access.

Who can see my account data?

Account auth is handled through Supabase services. See legal and privacy section for details.
