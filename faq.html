Frequently asked questions

The questions below now reflect the real current positioning of RiftSkin: macOS desktop app first, free core workflow, premium extras, and account use only where it makes sense.

Is the app usable for free?

Yes. Free mode keeps the core workflow available: import skins, browse the library, and run the game workflow.

What does premium unlock?

Premium unlocks favourite champions, favourite skins, and automatic champion recognition during pick phase.

Do I need to sign in inside the desktop app?

No. The desktop app works without a software login. Premium is activated with a code on the machine.

Do I still need the default skin in game?

Yes. For the replacement to apply correctly, select the champion default or base skin in game.

How do I get premium?

Subscribe from the web account page. Your subscription stays monthly at 15 EUR and you use the premium code in the app while the subscription is active.

Does RiftSkin support Windows?

Not in the current public distribution. The current supported workflow is focused on macOS.

Where do billing and account management happen?

Billing is managed on the website through your account flow. The desktop app remains focused on installation, imports, launch, and premium activation.

What should I send to support if something breaks?

Send your account email if relevant, your app version, the exact error message, and a screenshot if you have one. That shortens support time considerably.
