Frequently asked questions

FAQ 01

Can I use RIFTSKIN for free?

Yes. The free version lets you use the essential RIFTSKIN features: import your skins, browse your library, and launch your in-game injections, within a limit of 5 injections per day.

FAQ 02

What does Premium unlock?

Premium unlocks comfort and speed features: favourite champions, favourite skins, automatic champion recognition during champion select, and a higher number of injections.

FAQ 03

Do I need to sign in inside the app?

No. RIFTSKIN works without any required login inside the application. If you have Premium, you only need to enter your license number during app onboarding or from settings.

FAQ 04

Do I need to select the default skin in game?

Yes. For the replacement to work correctly, you must select the champion's base skin in game. If you choose another official skin, the replacement may not apply correctly.

FAQ 05

How do I get Premium?

You just need to subscribe on our site by creating an account. The subscription is 15 EUR per month. Once your subscription is active, you can enter your activation code inside the app.

FAQ 06

Is RIFTSKIN available on Windows?

No, not for now. The version currently available publicly is only compatible with macOS.

FAQ 07

What should I send to support if a problem occurs?

If you run into an issue, send a message from the Support tab on our site and include: the email address linked to your account, the app version visible at the top of the window when it is open, the error message if applicable, and a screenshot if possible. These details help us assist you faster. Our team will do its best to get back to you as quickly as possible.

FAQ 08

Is it safe to use RIFTSKIN?

At this time, custom skin usage is tolerated by Riot. However, we cannot guarantee that no policy change will happen in the future. Using RIFTSKIN is therefore done at your own risk.

FAQ 09

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account on our site.

FAQ 10

Will skin injection always work after a new League of Legends patch?

In principle, the injection system is not supposed to stop working after every new League of Legends patch. However, if a problem happens after an update, our team will step in quickly to restore the service as soon as possible.

FAQ 11

I want to subscribe, but I want to be sure injection still works with the latest patch. How can I verify that?

In the Support tab on our site, we display the current state of skin injection according to the latest League of Legends patch. If the indicator is shown in green, injection is currently working. If there is a bug, a message will be posted there to inform you and keep you updated on the progress of our fixes.
