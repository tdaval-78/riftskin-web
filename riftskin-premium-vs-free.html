RIFTSKIN Premium vs Free

The difference between free and Premium is not whether the product is usable or not. Free mode already covers the core workflow. Premium mainly speeds up daily use if you use custom skins on LoL Mac often.

What you already get in free mode

  • Import compatible custom skins.
  • Organized library on Mac.
  • Complete injection workflow.
  • Up to 5 injections per day.

What Premium adds

  • Unlimited injections.
  • Favorite champions.
  • Favorite skins.
  • Automatic champion recognition during pick phase.

When free mode is enough

Free mode is often enough if you are discovering RIFTSKIN, if you play occasionally, or if you simply want to confirm that the workflow fits your Mac before going further.

  • You are testing the product for the first time.
  • You do not need many injections per day.
  • You can live without favorites and pick-phase automation.

When Premium starts making sense

Premium becomes relevant mainly for regular users who want to remove as much friction as possible.

  • You use RIFTSKIN almost every day.
  • You want to remove the injection limit.
  • You play several champions and want to find your favorites quickly.
  • You want a more direct workflow during champion select.

The real gain of Premium

Premium does not change the product philosophy. It mostly removes micro-frictions: fewer manual actions, fewer round trips in the library, and more day-to-day speed. If you already use RIFTSKIN often, that is where the difference becomes tangible.

Frequent questions before moving to Premium

  • Premium is not needed to install the app, only to improve the workflow.
  • The web account is for billing and activation, not for locking free mode.
  • You can start free and move to Premium later without changing your workflow.
